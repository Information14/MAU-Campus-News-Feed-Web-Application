To check your result, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the university's official website at https://mautech.edu.ng

To activate your University email account, proceed with the following steps below:

  1. Go to gmail login using your browser or launch your Gmail mobile app
  2. In the email address box: type in your ID number, ignoring your department code and forward slash(/). Add @student.mau.edu.ng to it.
    Example:
    If your registration (ID) number is BCH/16U/2309, type in 16u2309@student.mau.edu.ng in the email-address input field.
  3. The default password is 12345678
  4. Ensure you change the default password in list 3, after you logged in for security measures.

For more clarification, visit the MAU Yola ICT department with your student ID card to activate your University email account.

Welcome to Modibbo Adama University

Modibbo Adama University (MAU), Yola, formerly known as Federal University of Technology, Yola, welcomes you warmly. By selecting MAU, you have become part of a tradition of excellence that has positioned the university in the spotlight locally, regionally, nationally, and internationally. Since its establishment in 1981, MAU Yola has consistently grown as a premier centre for teaching and research in Pure and Applied Sciences, Agriculture, Engineering, Management, Information Technology, and related disciplines.
MAU Yola takes pride in providing a serene university environment rich in experience, attracting a large number of applicants from Nigeria and neighbouring countries who seek admission into its academic programmes annually. You are therefore privileged to have been selected to study at this young yet potentially great university.

M.A.U Academic programs

Undergraduate Programme

Modibbo Adama University of Technology (MAU) offers a wide range of undergraduate programs in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Management. Prospective students can choose from 57 Bachelor's degree programs.
To be eligible, candidates must have a minimun of 5 credits at O level in relevant subjects which must include English language and mathematics for UTME and a minimum Point From UTME scores as can be fixed by the University Admission Comittee from time to time.
Post jamb screening is carried out for students seeking admission into the department as additional requirement by the University. Those Applying through Direct Entry (DE) should possess at least a lower credit in their 'A' Levels or Diploma Programmes or HND Programme in related and relevant fields. This is addition to possession of 5 credits at their 'O' Levels. For detailed program information and admission guidelines, visit https://mau.edu.ng/admission/undergraduate

Post Graduate Programme

Modibbo Adama University Yola offers a range of postgraduate programs leading to the award of Postgraduate Diplomas, Masters, and Ph.D. degrees. The programs are designed to provide advanced knowledge and skills in various fields, fostering academic excellence and research. Admission requirements include a minimum of a Bachelor's degree with a Second Class Lower Division from a recognized university, while some programs may require additional qualifications or professional experience. Candidates for Ph.D. programs must possess a Master's degree with a CGPA of 3.5 or higher. Applicants are also required to meet specific departmental requirements and pass a screening test or interview. For detailed program information and admission guidelines, visit https://mau.edu.ng/admission/postgraduate

Center for Distance Learning, Sandwich and Others

The Centre for Distance Learning (CDL) at Modibbo Adama University Yola provides opportunities for students to pursue undergraduate and postgraduate degrees through distance learning. The CDL offers programs in various fields, including Education, Management Sciences, and Social Sciences. The Sandwich program is a specialized program for teachers and other professionals, offered during school vacations. Admission requirements for CDL programs include a minimum of five O'Level credits, including English Language, while Sandwich program applicants must be practicing teachers or professionals with a minimum of five O'Level credits. Additional requirements may apply, depending on the program. For detailed program information and admission guidelines, visit https://mau.edu.ng/department/YqM8gpzr/center
